# Municipal Code § 937-3.5 — Closures of Black Creek Drive immediately north of Eglinton Avenue West to
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 937 (Temporary Closing of Highways), § 937-3.5. https://www.toronto.ca/legdocs/municode/1184_937.pdf

facilitate the construction of Eglinton Crosstown LRT elevated guideway.
[Added 2018-02-01 by By-law 148-2018]
Despite any other by-law and § 937-4, from February 1, 2018 to and including December 31,
2019, the General Manager of Transportation Services may temporarily partially close Black
Creek Drive immediately north of Eglinton Avenue West to vehicular and pedestrian traffic for
any period as required to facilitate the construction of Eglinton Crosstown LRT elevated
guideway and may fully close Black Creek Drive immediately north of Eglinton Avenue West to
vehicular and pedestrian traffic for up to and including ten (10) weekends to facilitate the
construction of Eglinton Crosstown LRT elevated guideway.
