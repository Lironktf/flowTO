# Toronto Municipal Code Chapter 950 — Traffic and Parking
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 950.
Temporary traffic regulation is permitted under an approved special-event Traffic
Management Plan (TMP). Closures, contraflow lanes, and signal retiming for a
sanctioned event (e.g. a stadium egress) are allowed when authorized through an
event TMP. Contraflow and temporary one-way operation on an arterial are valid
TMP measures provided emergency and transit access are preserved.
