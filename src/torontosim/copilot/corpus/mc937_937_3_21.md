# Municipal Code § 937-3.21 — Ontario Line Early Works in the Lakeshore East Joint Corridor
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 937 (Temporary Closing of Highways), § 937-3.21. https://www.toronto.ca/legdocs/municode/1184_937.pdf

[Added 2022-06-16 by By-law 590-2022]
Despite any other by-law, until December 31, 2026, inclusive, the General Manager of
Transportation Services may temporarily close to pedestrian and vehicular traffic highways or
portions of highways for a period up to and including 365 consecutive days but ending no later
than December 31, 2026, inclusive, as required for the purpose of the construction of the Ontario
Line Early Works in the Lakeshore East Joint Corridor.
