# Municipal Code § 937-3.7 — Closures of York Street between Bremner Boulevard and Lake Shore Boulevard
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 937 (Temporary Closing of Highways), § 937-3.7. https://www.toronto.ca/legdocs/municode/1184_937.pdf

West for the construction of the York Street PATH tunnel.
[Added 2018-05-24 by By-law 658-2018]
Despite any other by-law, until May 31, 2020 inclusive, the General Manager of Transportation
Services may temporarily close to vehicular and pedestrian traffic York Street between Bremner
Boulevard and Lake Shore Boulevard West, to a maximum of two lanes of traffic, for a period up
to and including 270 consecutive days but ending no later than May 31, 2020 inclusive, as
required for the purposes of construction of the York Street PATH tunnel.
