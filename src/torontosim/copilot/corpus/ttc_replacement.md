# TTC Service Bylaw — Streetcar Replacement Buses
Source: TTC service bylaw.
Where streetcar service (e.g. 509 Harbourfront / 511 Bathurst) is replaced or
supplemented by buses, a running lane must be retained for the replacement
buses. A full closure of Lake Shore Blvd W that removes the westbound lane
needed for 509/511 replacement routing is not permitted.
