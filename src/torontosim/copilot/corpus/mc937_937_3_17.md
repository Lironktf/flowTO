# Municipal Code § 937-3.17 — Eglinton Crosstown West Extension
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 937 (Temporary Closing of Highways), § 937-3.17. https://www.toronto.ca/legdocs/municode/1184_937.pdf

[Added 2021-02-05 by By-law 43-2021]
Despite any other by-law, until December 31, 2030 inclusive, the General Manager of
Transportation Services may temporarily close to pedestrian and vehicular traffic highways or
portions of highways for a period up to and including 365 consecutive days but ending no later
than December 31, 2030, inclusive, as required for the purpose of the construction of Eglinton
Crosstown West Extension.
