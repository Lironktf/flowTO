# Municipal Code § 743-49 — Fees for encroachments, street work and temporary street occupations
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 743 (Use of Streets and Sidewalks), § 743-49. https://www.toronto.ca/legdocs/municode/1184_743.pdf

A. With the exception of permitted encroachments as described in Article X, and unless
otherwise stipulated in an applicable agreement or statute, every person whose equipment
or encroachment is situated in, on, under, over, along, or across a street shall pay all
applicable fees and charges relating to the encroachment, street work, temporary street
occupation or RoDARS as described in Chapter 441, Fees and Charges. [Added 2025-
03-31 by By-law 190-202545]
B. Revenues collected by the General Manager pursuant to this chapter shall be applied to
the costs associated with managing and maintaining the City's streets.
C. The parameters of the area for the fees set out in reference numbers 38.1, 38.2, 38.3, 38.4,
38.5 and 38.6 of Appendix C - Schedule 2, Transportation Services, of Chapter 441, Fees
and Charges, shall be in accordance with the definition for the respective area as found in
§ 743-1A. [Added 2015-07-09 by By-law 813-2015
46]
