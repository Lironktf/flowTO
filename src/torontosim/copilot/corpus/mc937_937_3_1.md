# Municipal Code § 937-3.1 — Closure for purposes of 2021, 2022 and 2023 Honda Indy Toronto Race
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 937 (Temporary Closing of Highways), § 937-3.1. https://www.toronto.ca/legdocs/municode/1184_937.pdf

[Added 2003-02-07 by By-law 51-2003; amended 2005-05-19 by By-law 398-2005; 2009-04-
06 by By-law 410-2009; 2012-05-09 by By-law 638-2012; amended 2015-05-07 by By-
law 478-2015; 2016-04-01 by By-law 294-2016; 2018-03-28 by By-law 279-2018; 2021-03-01
by By-law 189-2021]
Despite any other by-law, the General Manager of Transportation Services of the City of Toronto
may temporarily close to vehicular and pedestrian traffic any highway or portion of a highway
for such periods as required for the purposes of the 2021, 2022 and 2023 Honda Indy Toronto
automobile race.
