# Toronto Municipal Code Chapter 880 — Fire Routes
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 880.
A designated fire route / emergency access corridor may not be fully closed to
all traffic. Interventions must maintain a clear emergency-vehicle path and fire-
route access lanes at all times. Closing the only emergency corridor to a venue
(e.g. Stadium South / Lake Shore Blvd W) in both directions is prohibited.
