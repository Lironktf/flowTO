# Municipal Code § 937-3.2 — Metrolinx Eglinton Crosstown LRT Project
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 937 (Temporary Closing of Highways), § 937-3.2. https://www.toronto.ca/legdocs/municode/1184_937.pdf

[Added 2016-04-01 by By-law 295-2016; amended 2022-05-12 by By-law 475-2022]
Despite any other by-law, until December 31, 2022, the General Manager of Transportation
Services may temporarily close to vehicular and pedestrian traffic any highway or portion of a
highway for a period up to and including 365 consecutive days as required for the purposes of
the construction of the Metrolinx Eglinton Crosstown LRT Project.
2 Editor's Note:  Section 937-2.1. entitled "Metrolinx Eglinton Crosstown Project" was deleted April 1, 2016 by By-law 294-2016.
