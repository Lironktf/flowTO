# Municipal Code § 937-3.6 — Closures of King Street West and King Street East for the King Street Transit
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 937 (Temporary Closing of Highways), § 937-3.6. https://www.toronto.ca/legdocs/municode/1184_937.pdf

Pilot - Outdoor Cafes and Public Installations in the Curb Lane Public Spaces.
[Added 2018-03-27 by By-law 338-2018; amended 2018-12-13 by By-law 62-2019; 2019-04-
17 by By-law 587-2019; amended 2019-11-27 by By-law 1652-2019]
Despite any other by-law, until April 14, 2023, the General Manager of Transportation Services
may temporarily close to vehicular traffic portions of the curb lanes on King Street West and
King Street East for any length of time but ending no later than April 14, 2023 or the King Street
Transit Pilot - Outdoor Cafes and Public Installations in the Curb Lane Public Spaces.
