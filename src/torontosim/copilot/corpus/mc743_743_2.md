# Municipal Code § 743-2 — Sale of event tickets
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 743 (Use of Streets and Sidewalks), § 743-2. https://www.toronto.ca/legdocs/municode/1184_743.pdf

[Amended 2004-04-16 by By-law 274-2004]
No person shall use or occupy a street for the purposes of the sale, or offering for sale, of event
tickets.
