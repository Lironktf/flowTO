# Municipal Code § 937-3.15 — Line 2 Scarborough Subway Extension
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 937 (Temporary Closing of Highways), § 937-3.15. https://www.toronto.ca/legdocs/municode/1184_937.pdf

[Added 2020-01-29 by By-law 116-2020]
Despite any other by-law, until December 31, 2030 inclusive, the General Manager of
Transportation Services may temporarily close to pedestrian and vehicular traffic highways or
portions of highways for a period up to and including 365 consecutive days but ending no later
than December 31, 2030, inclusive, as required for the purpose of the construction of the Line 2
Scarborough Subway Extension.
