Notwithstanding §§ 937-1 to 937-3.1, inclusive, and § 937-3.19, the General Manager of
Transportation Services shall, prior to approving any temporary street closure under §§ 937-1 to
937-3.1 or under § 937-3.19, notify the ward councillor(s) in whose ward the road closure is
pending of the pending closure. If requested by the affected ward's councillor, the General
Manager of Transportation Services shall not approve the temporary street closure but shall
report the matter to the appropriate community council for final decision under delegated
authority, or for recommendation to Council for final decision.
