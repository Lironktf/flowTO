# Municipal Code § 950-203 — Regulation for Micro-Utility Device
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 950 (Traffic and Parking), § 950-203. https://www.toronto.ca/legdocs/municode/1184_950.pdf

[Added 2021-12-17 by By-law 1075-2021]
A. No person shall operate a micro-utility device on a sidewalk.
B. No person shall park, store or leave a micro-utility device on a highway or sidewalk.
