# Municipal Code § 743-52 — Conflicting provisions
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 743 (Use of Streets and Sidewalks), § 743-52. https://www.toronto.ca/legdocs/municode/1184_743.pdf

[Amended 2006-05-25 by By-law 462-2006; 2010-05-12 by By-law 482-2010
51; 2012-03-07
by By-law 375-201252]
In the case of any conflict between this chapter and any by-law of the former Borough of East
York, the former Cities of Etobicoke, North York, Toronto, Scarborough or York, or the former
Municipality of Metropolitan Toronto, this chapter shall prevail.
