[Added 2023-05-15 by By-law 451-2023]
Despite any other by-law, the General Manager of Transportation Services may, to facilitate the
geotechnical monitoring and measurement work related to the Ontario Line Don Valley Crossing,
temporarily close to vehicular traffic:

between 600 metres south of Beechwood Drive and 350 metres north of the Leaside
Bridge, from 11:00 p.m. of one day to 5:00 a.m. on the following day for one night
between September 1, 2023 and September 30, 2023, inclusive;

Parkway, between 450 metres north of the Leaside Bridge and 300 metres south of
Beechwood Drive, from 11:00 p.m. of one day to 5:00 a.m. on the following day for one
night between September 1, 2023 and September 30, 2023, inclusive;

between November 1, 2023 and November 30, 2023, inclusive;

Parkway, between 450 metres north of the Leaside Bridge and 300 metres south of
Beechwood Drive, from 11:00 p.m. of one day to 5:00 a.m. on the following day for one
night between November 1, 2023 and November 30, 2023, inclusive;
E. The northbound outside lane and adjacent pavement shoulder on the Don Valley Parkway,

between April 1, 2024 and April 30, 2024, inclusive;
