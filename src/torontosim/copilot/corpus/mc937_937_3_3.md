# Municipal Code § 937-3.3 — Closures of the W.R. Allen Road for the Metrolinx Eglinton Crosstown LRT
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 937 (Temporary Closing of Highways), § 937-3.3. https://www.toronto.ca/legdocs/municode/1184_937.pdf

Project.
[Added 2016-07-15 by By-law 768-2016]
Despite any other by-law and § 937-4, from July 18, 2016 to and including January 1, 2018, the
General Manager, Transportation Services may temporarily close to vehicular and pedestrian
traffic the W.R. Allen Road, including its entry and exit roadway ramps, for a period of up to and
including 4 consecutive days on up to and including 12 occasions as required for the purposes of
construction of the Metrolinx Eglinton Crosstown LRT project.
