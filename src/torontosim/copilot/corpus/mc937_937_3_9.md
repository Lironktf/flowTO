# Municipal Code § 937-3.9 — Metrolinx Regional Express Rail and SmartTrack Projects
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 937 (Temporary Closing of Highways), § 937-3.9. https://www.toronto.ca/legdocs/municode/1184_937.pdf

[Added 2018-05-24 by By-law 660-2018]
Despite any other by-law, the General Manager of Transportation Services may temporarily
close to vehicular and pedestrian traffic highways or portions of highways for a period up to and
including 365 consecutive days, until March 31, 2025, as required for the purposes of the
construction of the Metrolinx Regional Express Rail and SmartTrack projects.
