# Municipal Code § 937-3.10 — Closures of Black Creek Drive between Photography Drive and Eglinton
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 937 (Temporary Closing of Highways), § 937-3.10. https://www.toronto.ca/legdocs/municode/1184_937.pdf

Avenue West for the construction of the Eglinton Crosstown LRT elevated guideway.
[Added 2018-05-24 by By-law 660-20183]
Despite any other by-law and § 937-4, from May 23, 2018 to and including December 31, 2019,
to facilitate the construction of the Eglinton Crosstown LRT elevated guideway, the General
Manager may temporarily partially close to pedestrian and vehicular traffic Black Creek Drive
between Photography Drive and Eglinton Avenue West for any period as required and may
temporarily fully close to pedestrian and vehicular traffic Black Creek Drive between
Photography Drive and Eglinton Avenue West for up to 72 consecutive hours for any number of
occasions.
