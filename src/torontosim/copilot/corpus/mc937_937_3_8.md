# Municipal Code § 937-3.8 — Metrolinx Finch West LRT corridor
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 937 (Temporary Closing of Highways), § 937-3.8. https://www.toronto.ca/legdocs/municode/1184_937.pdf

[Added 2018-05-24 by By-law 660-2018; amended 2019-05-15 by By-law 787-2019]
Despite any other by-law, the General Manager of Transportation Services may temporarily close
to vehicular and pedestrian traffic highways or portions of highways for a period up to and
including 365 consecutive days, until August 31, 2023, as required for the purposes of the
construction of the Metrolinx Finch West LRT corridor.
