# Municipal Code § 886-6 — Paths established
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 886 (Bicycle Lanes and Cycle Tracks), § 886-6. https://www.toronto.ca/legdocs/municode/1184_886.pdf

A. The portions of highways listed in Schedule C to this chapter are established as bicycle
paths.
B. Despite the provisions of any other City by-law to the contrary, any person may ride a
bicycle on a bicycle path.
