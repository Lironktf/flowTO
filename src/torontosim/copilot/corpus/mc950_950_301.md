# Municipal Code § 950-301 — Pedestrians prohibited on certain highways
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 950 (Traffic and Parking), § 950-301. https://www.toronto.ca/legdocs/municode/1184_950.pdf

A. Subject to Subsection B, pedestrians are prohibited from using those parts of the
highways set out in Schedule II in § 950-1301 between the limits set out in Schedule II.
B. Subsection A does not apply to pedestrians engaged in police duties, highway
maintenance or construction duties or where, owing to an emergency, it is necessary to
make use of a highway.
