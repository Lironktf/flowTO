# Municipal Code § 937-3.18 — Toronto Transit Commission's Easier Access Phase Three Projects
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 937 (Temporary Closing of Highways), § 937-3.18. https://www.toronto.ca/legdocs/municode/1184_937.pdf

[Added 2021-04-08 by By-law 217-2021]
Despite any other by-law, until December 31, 2025 inclusive, the General Manager of
Transportation Services may temporarily close to pedestrian and vehicular traffic highways or
portions of highways, with the exception of any highway or portion of highway in Ward 14 –
Toronto-Danforth, for a period up to and including 365 consecutive days but ending no later than
December 31, 2025, inclusive, as required for the purpose of the construction of the Toronto
Transit Commission's Easier Access Phase Three Projects.
