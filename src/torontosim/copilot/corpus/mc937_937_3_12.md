# Municipal Code § 937-3.12 — Closures of Highway 27 for the Finch West LRT Project
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 937 (Temporary Closing of Highways), § 937-3.12. https://www.toronto.ca/legdocs/municode/1184_937.pdf

[Added 2019-05-15 by By-law 787-2019; amended 2019-10-03 by By-law 1386-2019;
amended 2020-05-28 by By-law 381-2020]
Despite any other by-law and § 937-4, until August 31, 2023 inclusive, the General Manager of
Transportation Services may temporarily close to pedestrian and vehicular traffic portions of
Highway 27, between 500 metres north of Finch Avenue West and 500 metres south of Humber
College Boulevard, for a period up to and including 365 consecutive days but ending no later
than August 31, 2023, inclusive, as required for the purposes of construction of the Finch West
Light Rail Transit.
