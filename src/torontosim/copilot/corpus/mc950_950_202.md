# Municipal Code § 950-202 — Regulation for e-scooters
Source: City of Toronto Municipal Code, Ch. 950 (Traffic and Parking), § 950-202. https://www.toronto.ca/legdocs/municode/1184_950.pdf

[Added 2019-10-03 by By-law 1405-2019]
A. No person shall operate an e-scooter on a sidewalk.
B. No person shall park, store or leave an e-scooter on a highway or sidewalk.
